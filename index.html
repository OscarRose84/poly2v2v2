Polytopia 2v2v2 Team Picker

Players generate a “token” from their coordinates. The token is not plain text coordinates. Organiser pastes 6 tokens to compute 3 teams of 2, prioritising fairness so no team gets a disaster pairing at spawn.

Shared settings (everyone must match)

Coordinates can be an estimate from a screenshot grid. Just be consistent.

Player: generate your token

Send ONLY the token to the organiser.

Organiser: paste 6 tokens
